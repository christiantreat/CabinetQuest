📚 Getting Started

Welcome to the Teacher Admin Panel! Use this interface to customize every aspect of the Trauma Room Trainer:

  • Room Layout: Customize cart positions, colors, dimensions, and background
  • Scenarios: Create training scenarios with custom feedback and scoring
  • Drawers: Configure drawer layouts for each cart
  • Items: Add medical items with custom images and descriptions
  • Achievements: Design achievement criteria and scoring rules
