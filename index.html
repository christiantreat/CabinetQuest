Cabinet Organization Game

Welcome to the Cabinet Organization Game! Here's how to play:


1. You'll be given different missions to complete

2. Each mission involves collecting specific items from cabinets

3. Click on cabinets to open drawers

4. Click on items to collect them into your inventory

5. Click on items in your inventory to return them

6. Submit your mission when you think you've completed it


Complete all missions to win the game!
